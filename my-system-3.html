📱 External saw something / scroll
  • 1
    Phone down immediately
  • 2
    Relocate — different room or outside
  • 3
    Start a task using hands or full attention
  • 4
    No negotiating. Don't finish the scroll first.
🧠 Internal boredom / loneliness
  • 1
    Don't fight it. Don't run.
  • 2
    Sit still. Notice where you feel it.
  • 3
    Breathe. Observe it like a wave.
  • 4
    Wait it out — 10 to 15 minutes max.
💭 Memory Hit replay of scene / person
  • 1
    Label it — "my brain wants a hit, that's all"
  • 2
    Don't suppress it. Don't replay it.
  • 3
    Redirect outward — music, gym, convo, podcast
  • 4
    Expect it to return. Same response every time.
🚩 "One Time" Thought red flag
  • 1
    That thought = you're already in the red zone
  • 2
    Don't engage or argue with it
  • 3
    Apply whichever rule above fits — NOW
🔄 It Comes Back
  • 1
    Start the rule over. Same process.
  • 2
    Every repeat you survive is a WIN — not a failure.
📲 Really Struggling
  • 1
    Text your accountability partner. Right now. Not after.
